Note from Justin: The student notes that the homeworks "hand hold", and I believe they are picking up on some of the intentional "scaffolding" I put in place when designing homeworks. I often start with a very challenging problem that requires the student to explore unseen concepts or tackle tough computations. I then break the problem into subproblems that outline the correct strategy. To get the overall correct anwer, however, the student not only needs to think about each subproblem individually, but how they fit together.
