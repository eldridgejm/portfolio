Redemption Exams

The redemption exams will be available on Wednesday, June 10 as quizzes on Canvas. As with the midterms, you will be able to start the quiz at any time on that day. Each redemption exam will be its own quiz, and you'll be able to take one, both, or neither of them. Your score on the redemption exam will replace your score on the corresponding midterm only if it is higher (and so the redemption exams are effectively optional).

Each quiz will have its own independent time limit of 1 hour and 30 minutes. The syllabus originally stated that you will be given 4 hours for the final exam, but I've been made aware of a university rule saying that online exams must be 3 hours or less. In light of this, I'm going to make the redemption exams a little shorter than the midterms so that you should have ample time to complete them. While you will have 1.5 hours for each exam, they will be designed to take less than 1 hour.

Midterm review videos are available below:

  • Midterm 01
  • Midterm 02
Reviewing these videos is considered excellent preparation for the redemption exams.
