Homeworks

Homeworks will be due on Fridays at 5:00 p.m. You can submit your homework via Gradescope. The late period extends 24 hours past the due date. At the end of the quarter, your tokens are automatically used to turn late homeworks into on-time homeworks.

You can work in pairs on the homework — just be sure to turn in a single assignment on Gradescope as a team.
