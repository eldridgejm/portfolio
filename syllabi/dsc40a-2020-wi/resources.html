Final Exam Practice

Linked below are several exams from previous quarters (some with solutions, some without). You may find these exams useful when studying, however, please note that they are not indicative of the format of this quarter's final exam! This is partly due to the fact that our exam will be online, and therefore necessarily of a different format. But it is also due to the fact that the content of DSC 40A has changed slightly over the previous two years. So while the exams are mostly similar, some will contain questions about topics we haven't discussed, such as PCA or k-Means clustering.
